Dog Interaction Guidelines

We have a reactive dog in the party space, so please be cautious around her.

  • Please do not knock on the door when you arrive.
  • There will be treats by the door, so come on in and be ready to throw them on the ground for her.
  • Do not lean over or reach quickly toward the dog.
  • Do not attempt to pet her!
  • If the dog seems uncomfortable, give her space and step back.
  • If she brings you a toy, she wants you to throw it! You've just made a friend!
    (But still don't try to pet her)

She is very nervous around strangers, but playing catch/fetch will help her feel more comfortable.


Thank you for helping keep the space safe and comfortable for everyone (especially Billie!).
